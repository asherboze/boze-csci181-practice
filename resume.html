About Me

I was born in Michigan but I grew up in Scobey, Montana. There, I learned to love coding, music, and life. In High School, I took a python class from Helena, which introduced me to coding. I also performed a few sets at the local brewery. I play trombone, guitar, and sing. I know Python and am in the process of learning HTML.

Education

I graduated from Scobey High School with Valedictorian status and am currently attending the University of Montana as an honors student, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Voice.

Professional Experience

My sophomore year, I worked as a volunteer at the Fort Peck Summer Theatre as an actor. I worked for my parents and helped them build a house near Fort Peck last winter break.
